Water Hyacinth: Description
The Water Hyacinth is a stem of many violet flowers which are accompanied by many broad leaves. The flower petals are spiky, and gradient from a light lavender on the outer parts of the petal to a darker violet and yellow spot in the center. The plant has dark, thin, but densely abundant roots.
